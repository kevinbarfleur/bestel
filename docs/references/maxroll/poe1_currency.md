# Maxroll — Currency Catalog (Path of Exile 1)

Source: <https://maxroll.gg/poe/category/currency>

This catalog covers Maxroll's currency-farming articles, divided into three groups:

1. **League-Start Strategies** — low-investment farms designed to bootstrap a fresh-league character to mapping wealth.
2. **Endgame Strategies** — mid-to-high investment farms that scale strongly with character power.
3. **Mechanic Deep-Dives** — reference articles explaining a specific league mechanic in depth.

The current PoE1 league at the time of this doc is **Mirage 3.28**. Strategy articles update per league. Always verify the patch version Maxroll updated for and cross-check with the player's actual league.

---

## League-Start Strategies

These are designed for the first 1–7 days of a fresh league when the economy is lean and player gear is poor. They focus on low investment, fast turnover, and selling commodities that have peak demand at league start.

### 1. League Start Alch and Go

**URL:** <https://maxroll.gg/poe/currency/league-start-alch-and-go>

**What it covers:** The default beginner strategy — alch (use Orb of Alchemy) every map and run it. Focuses on speedy Atlas progression toward 4 Voidstones. The Atlas Tree skeleton emphasises Essence drops + map sustain nodes. Article covers gameplay flow, Atlas point allocation priority, what to sell (Essences upgraded to Shrieking, basic currency stack — Annul/Alch/Alteration/Fusing/Horizons/Regret/Scouring/Unmaking/Regal/Vaal). Strong recommendation to focus on speed over juicing during this phase.

**When to consult:** Player just started a fresh league, asks "what should I do first," doesn't know how to allocate early Atlas points, or asks "do I need scarabs to start."

**Recency notes:** Updated for 3.28 Mirage — Atlas tree screenshot may shift between leagues.

### 2. Legion League Start Strategy

**URL:** <https://maxroll.gg/poe/currency/legion>

**What it covers:** Low-investment Legion farm that rewards high clear speed. Uses Domination Shrines and Niko Sulphite buffs to clear Legion encounters. Targets various currencies from Legion chests and rare monsters. Best paired with high-AoE / high-clear-speed builds. Sell Timeless Emblems in full sets via Currency Exchange, sell Incubators via empty-tab bulk listings. Notes: Patch 3.28 introduced 3 new incubators that drop from Legions within Mirage. Endgame variant is Eternal Conflict Legion Farming.

**When to consult:** Player asks for a clear-speed-friendly league start strategy, wants Legion as their go-to mechanic, or asks how to monetize Timeless Emblems.

### 3. Breach League Start Strategy

**URL:** <https://maxroll.gg/poe/currency/breach-league-start-strategy>

**What it covers:** Low-investment farm focused on generating Hiveblood and Wombgifts (4 types: Ancient, Mysterious, Lavish, Provisioning) that can be exchanged at the Genesis Tree for high-value rare items and Foulborn Uniques, or sold raw. Favours high clear speed builds that can handle multiple unstable Breaches. Article covers the Genesis Tree decision (which tree to consume Wombgifts on depends on character needs and meta).

**When to consult:** Player asks about Breach as a league starter, doesn't know what a Wombgift is, or asks about the Genesis Tree mechanic.

**Recency notes:** Added for Patch 3.28 — Mirage introduced these mechanics.

### 4. Ritual League Start Strategy

**URL:** <https://maxroll.gg/poe/currency/ritual>

**What it covers:** Low-investment, high-variance strategy focused on generating Tribute to withdraw items from Ritual pages at end of map. High variance — many maps between jackpots. Recommended map: City Square (3 map bosses = significantly more tribute per map). Article notes the Blood-filled Vessel improves overall reward quality but eliminates Ritual Splinter drops and prevents using own Vessels.

**When to consult:** Player asks about Ritual farming, asks what Tribute is, or wants a low-effort mechanic.

### 5. Settlers League Start Strategy

**URL:** <https://maxroll.gg/poe/currency/settlers>

**What it covers:** Low-investment Kingsmarch / Settlers of Kalguur strategy. Farms Ore for maximum-value shipments. Runs Niko for Sulphite Veins (Refiner's Bargain Atlas passive). Multiplied by speedrunning maps. Includes Smuggler's Caches and Memory Threads as side mechanics. Patch 3.28 buffed shipments — Ore-filled shipments now return Currency items (instead of Equipment), and Kingsmarch workers smelt Ores into Bars at 2x speed. Mining → Smelting (5x shipment value). Starfall Crater encounter has small chance to drop Svalinn (extremely valuable). In Mirage, Mirages have a chance to include Settlers encounter awarding Thaumaturgic Dust.

**When to consult:** Player asks about Kingsmarch, asks how to make currency from shipments, or asks "is Settlers good in 3.28."

**Recency notes:** Heavily updated for 3.28. The shipment economy shifted significantly.

### 8. Destructive Play League Start Strategy

**URL:** <https://maxroll.gg/poe/currency/destructive-play-currency-strategy>

## Endgame Strategies (Mid-to-High Investment)

These scale strongly with character power, currency investment, and Atlas Tree commitment. Designed for players who have completed their Atlas and are ready to specialise.

### 9. Map Farming Strategy

**URL:** <https://maxroll.gg/poe/currency/endgame-strategies/map-farming-strategy>

**What it covers:** Low-investment endgame farm producing maximum Nightmare Maps and Memory-influenced Maps. Uses Originator-influenced maps to combine "More Maps" modifier with Nightmare drops. Best map: Dunes (open layout, easy boss access). Drops to sell: Nightmare Maps (high-priority listing in dedicated tab), 8-mod corrupted maps with high "More Maps" rolls (start at 1 Divine, reduce over time), Originator Maps (require completing Originator questline by defeating Incarnation of Dread).

**When to consult:** Player asks how to sustain maps, how to make currency from selling maps, or asks about Nightmare Map farming. Also for "what's the best low-investment endgame farm."

**Recency notes:** Highly meta-specific to 3.28 — the Nightmare/Originator interaction is current-meta-dependent.

### 10. Kalguuran Map Farming

**URL:** <https://maxroll.gg/poe/currency/kalguuran-map-farming>

**What it covers:** Map farming using common scarabs to upgrade normal monsters around ores into magic monsters, then manipulating Crimson Ore spawn mechanics for additional monster spawns. Best map: Jungle Valley (no boss altars). Roll for 70%+ More Maps. Side benefit: significant Ore acquisition for Kingsmarch shipments. Patch 3.28 made this much stronger via shipment buffs and 2x smelt speed. The fifth scarab slot — Scarab of Monstrous Lineage is the recommended slot. Fully complete Originator questline to drop Originator Maps.

**When to consult:** Player asks for an endgame map farm with Kingsmarch synergy, asks how Crimson Ore spawn manipulation works, or asks about scarab combinations for map farming.

**Recency notes:** Patch 3.28 buffs heavily favoured this strategy. Verify current state.

### 11. Eternal Conflict Legion Farming

**URL:** <https://maxroll.gg/poe/currency/eternal-conflict-legion-farming>

**What it covers:** Mid-investment loot-tile farming via the Legion Scarab of Eternal Conflict and Legion Scarab of Treasures. The latter gives Legion chests a 20% chance to spread their rewards to surrounding monsters they release from stasis. Eternal Conflict prevents true breakouts until the very end, allowing Treasures to consistently apply. Best map: Dunes. Heavily rewards high clear speed builds. Mirage Legion encounters are weighted toward Maraketh Splinters and can drop 3 new Mirage incubators (the most sought-after).

**When to consult:** Player asks for an endgame Legion farm, wants to farm Maraketh Splinters, or asks about loot-tile spreading mechanics.

### 12. Unstable Breach Currency Farming

**URL:** <https://maxroll.gg/poe/currency/unstable-breach-currency-farming>

**What it covers:** Difficult, late-game, high-investment farm — described as the highest raw Currency returns in the game. Uses Horned Scarab of Bloodlines + Breach Atlas passives to spawn thousands of magic monsters per Breach encounter. Best map: Dunes (open layout). Uses Grasping Astrolabe (rewards scale with consecutive map count). Drops Mirror of Kalandra, raw currency at scale. In Mirage Breach encounters can drop Foulborn Afarud Uniques (chase items).

**When to consult:** Player asks for the highest-currency endgame farm, asks about Mirror chase, or wants to know about Grasping Astrolabe stacking.

### 13. Big Boom Expedition Farming

**URL:** <https://maxroll.gg/poe/currency/big-boom-expedition-farming>

**What it covers:** Most difficult Expedition variant. Detonates the entire fully juiced Expedition with one massive explosion via Extreme Archaeology Atlas keystone + Expedition Scarab of Verisium Powder. Best for DoT builds (single big tick). Best map: Dunes. Remnants apply both positive and negative modifiers across the entire encounter (instead of sequentially), drastically increasing both danger and loot. Eater of Worlds influence + The Shadow of Hunger combo recommended.

**When to consult:** Player asks how to scale Expedition further, asks about Extreme Archaeology, or has a DoT build looking for a synergy farm.

### 14. Magic Alva Gold Farming Strategy

**URL:** <https://maxroll.gg/poe/currency/magic-alva-gold-farming-strategy>

**What it covers:** Gold-targeted farm using Incursion Scarab of Champions + Time Dilation to spawn dense magic monster packs in Incursion, combined with Horned Scarab of Glittering and Bismuth Ore (adds modifiers to magic monsters). Difficulty spike from Bismuth activation. Best map: Jungle Valley. Templar Astrolabe recommended (Southwest vault rewards). Expects ~1 million gold/hour in Mirage 3.28. Crimson Ore manipulation (50% pod destruction triggers blooms with extra mob spawns) is critical.

**When to consult:** Player asks how to farm gold for Kingsmarch, asks about Incursion + Bismuth synergy, or asks about Crimson Ore optimization.

## Mechanic Deep-Dive Guides

These articles teach a single mechanic in depth, independent of any specific strategy. Use these to explain how a mechanic works rather than how to optimize it.

### 25. Harvest Farming Guide

**URL:** <https://maxroll.gg/poe/currency/harvest-farming-guide>

**What it covers:** Full primer on Harvest. Sacred Grove access (added to map), three Lifeforce colours (Vivid/Primal/Wild — different crafts unlocked per colour), plot selection between two random plots, high-rarity plant tier mechanics, Sacred Blossom drops from rare unique seeds (boss fight access). Eldritch Altar synergy (Item Quantity altars massively increase profits). Harvest Scarab of Doubling (must-use 2x multiplier for Lifeforce). Harvest Scarab of Cornucopia (guaranteed Tier 4 seeds, niche use).

**When to consult:** Player asks how Harvest works, asks what Lifeforce is for, or asks how to choose plots. Also for "how do I scale Harvest profit."

### 26. Essence Farming Guide

**URL:** <https://maxroll.gg/poe/currency/essence-farming-guide>

**What it covers:** Primer on Essence. Critical note: Essences don't scale with map tier beyond T6 — same rewards in white T6 as red T16. This makes Essence one of the best early-Atlas farms. Section on combining with other mechanics (Bestiary, Heist) since Essence works in any tier. Vaal Orb corruption on purple Essences (MEDS). Atlas passives are a major boost — full breakdown of which to pick. Includes Einhar's Memory of Crystal Prisons (Atlas Memory with Rogue Exiles imprisoned in Essences — extremely dangerous but profitable, Yellow T6 maps without Voidstones recommended).

**When to consult:** Player asks how Essence farming works, asks why Essences don't scale with map tier, or asks about Atlas Memory of Crystal Prisons.

---

### 27. Breach Farming Guide

**URL:** <https://maxroll.gg/poe/currency/breach-farming-guide>

**What it covers:** Primer on Breach. Five Breach types (Tul/Esh/Xoph/Uul-Netol/Chayula), each drops corresponding Splinter (100 splinters → full Breachstone). Breach Hands (regular + Breach Lord named — open Breach Lord hands first for max splinters + chance at The Chosen divination card → Skin of the Lords body armor). Breach Lord encounter chance scales with Atlas passives. Note: divination card The Chosen can be turned in for Skin of the Lords (high variance gamble).

**When to consult:** Player asks how Breach works, asks what Breach Splinters are, or asks about Skin of the Lords.

### 28. Expedition Farming Guide

### 29. Legion Farming Guide

### 30. Betrayal Farming Guide

**URL:** <https://maxroll.gg/poe/currency/betrayal-farming-guide>

**What it covers:** Primer on Betrayal / Immortal Syndicate. Jun in maps spawns 3 encounters (Research + Intervention always, third is Transportation or Fortification). 14 of 16 possible Syndicate members on board. Choices: Interrogate (always available, -1 rank, +intel for 3 encounters), Execute (only if witnessed by another member, +1 rank), Bargain (currency / veiled items / division swap / recruit), Betray (turn 2 members into Rivals — powerful for board manipulation). Safehouse mechanics (100% intel triggers entry; defeating leader drops Syndicate Medallion or Veiled Chaos Orb; rewards depend on member assignments). Mastermind = Catarina (see Bosses catalog).

**When to consult:** Player asks how Betrayal works, asks how to set up Syndicate Board, asks what Veiled Chaos Orbs are for, or asks how to farm Catarina.

## Cross-reference notes

- **For ALL strategy articles:** Mirage 3.28 is the current league. Anything with specific scarabs, atlas trees, or astrolabe synergies will shift in 3.29+. Always verify the patch tag in the article header.
- **Scarab system:** Many of these strategies reference specific scarabs (Cornucopia, Verisium Powder, Eternal Conflict, etc.). The full scarab list is on poewiki.net; Maxroll references them in the context of strategies.
- **Atlas Tree:** Most strategies include Atlas Passive Tree screenshots. These are the most volatile per-league element.
- **Astrolabes:** New 3.28 mechanic. Each strategy specifies which Astrolabe to use (Grasping for Breach, Fruiting for Harvest, Enshrouded for Delirium, Runic for Expedition, Templar for Incursion-gold, Timeless for Eternal Conflict Legion).
- **Mirage encounters:** New 3.28 mechanic adding parallel "Mirage areas" with empowered league mechanics. Many strategies have Mirage-specific drop notes.
