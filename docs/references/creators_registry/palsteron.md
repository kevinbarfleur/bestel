## Identity

Specialises in *bow and totem* builds. Writing is unusually clear about itemisation philosophy.

> "The Item Progression in Path of Exile is not as rigid as in most other games... flexibility is key. The game won't always give you exactly the item you are looking for, so you need to adapt. Even if small upgrades seem insignificant, the sum of them decides whether your character fails or succeeds."
