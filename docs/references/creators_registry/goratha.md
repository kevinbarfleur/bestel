# Goratha

- **PoE1 profile**: <https://maxroll.gg/@goratha?game=poe>
- **PoE2 profile**: <https://maxroll.gg/@goratha?game=poe2>

## Identity

Maxroll content creator. Build philosophy: a build should be **playable from level 1 with no twink gear**, scale gracefully through the campaign, and remain viable through Atlas progression without requiring a chase unique to function.

## Known archetypes

- **PoE1**: Penance Brand Ignite Elementalist, Rolling Magma Mines Saboteur, Shock Nova of Procession Archmage Hierophant, Eviscerate Bleed Gladiator.
- **PoE2 (0.4+)**: Disciple of Varashta plant builds, Fireball Blood Mage, Bleed Rake Amazon.

## Signature quirks

- League-start friendly; campaign progression always present.
- SSF-aware budget assumptions.
- Multi-tier progression tabs typical.

## Biases / known gaps

- Less focused on uber-pinnacle peak optimisation.
- Trade-only mirror-tier guides not his focus.

Always open the Maxroll profile URL to retrieve the current build list — guides are versioned per league.
